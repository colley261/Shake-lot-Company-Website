About Mrs. Colley

Josee Colley is an experienced drama teacher. She holds a B.Ed degree in English and Drama, and has recently completed an MA in ‘The Advanced Teaching of Shakespeare’ at Warwick University in the UK. She has taught Shakespeare in an active, participatory way to non- English speaking Primary students in the New Territories. She also has experience in working with middle and high school students.
