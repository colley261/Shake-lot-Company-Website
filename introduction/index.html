Official Website

Josee Colley is an experienced drama teacher. She holds a B.Ed degree in English and Drama and has recently completed an MA in ‘The Advanced Teaching of Shakespeare’ at Warwick University in the UK. She has taught Shakespeare in an active, participatory way to none- English speaking Primary students in the New Territories. She also has experience in working with Middle- and high school students.

Why choose this programme?

In today's growing society, having a range of skills will aid you or your students/children land a job, as they need an advantage over the other people applying for the same job. Shakespeare is one of the most well-known forms of drama in modern literature, so understanding the plays and text used is extremely important if you attempt to land a job in almost any field. Mrs Colley, who has an MA in the teaching of Shakespeare, is the best choice for learning Shakespeare in a friendly and affordable environment.

Why learn Shakespeare

Shakespeare is a classic form of performing arts, and learning his works is a great experience for anyone looking to go into the film/performing industry.
