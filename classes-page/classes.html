Workshops We Offer

Here at the Shake@lot Company, we offer various class options. Here, you can browse the classes before making an appointment for yourself or your institution. Need help deciding what class fits your needs? Visit the contacts page to recieve help from our staff so you can identify the perfect plan for you!

There are two main groups of classes you can book; Singular workshops and Weekly workshops. However, pricing will vary for these classes depending on where you are booking these classes. The three choices you have here are as follows; Primary Schools, Secondary Schools or Tertiary Institutions. More information on the choices can be found below.

Primary Schools

This option for classes is for workshops based in schools. This will mean the workshop will be suitable from Year 1 all the way to Year 6. Students only need basic english for these classes. They will also engage in interesting activities that will help them grasp the concept of the course.

Secondary Schools

The course for secondary schools is a far more complex and complicated course compared to the primary course. Students will require a higher understanding of english, as the course will contain language that some participants may not understand, therefore a higher knowledge of english will be required for this workshop. However, the majority of activities will be the same.

Tietary Institutions

Tietary institution workshops include the same activities as the secondary schools, although the english required will be much higher.
